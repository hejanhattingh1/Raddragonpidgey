Raddragonpidgey (Rad)

A Dragonal capable of gravity manipulation, existing in both human and dragonal forms in a world reshaped by universal collapse.

Overview

Raddragonpidgey, commonly known as Rad, is a member of the Dragonal race — humans capable of shapeshifting into dragon-based hybrid forms. Rad’s transformation results in a wingless half-dragon, half-griffin body supported by advanced gravity manipulation.

He inhabits a post-collapse world where multiple universes fused into one, scattering corrupted shards that infect living beings. As a member of Mytharxsis, Rad works to stop the spread of corruption and protect untainted life.

    Abilities

    Rad possesses gravity manipulation that functions identically in both forms. The transformation affects physical capability, not power origin.

    • Directional gravity control (including sideways falling)
    • Object gravity alteration through contact
    • Localized gravity amplification or reduction

    Statistics

    World & Lore

    A universal collapse fused multiple realities into one, raining corrupted shards that infect living beings. Corrupted entities instinctively attack anything uncorrupted, destabilizing what remains of civilization.

    Mytharxsis

    Mytharxsis is a leaderless, found-family alliance formed in response to the corruption. Members are bound by trust rather than hierarchy. Rad is an active member who uses his abilities to confront corrupted threats directly.

    Trivia

    • Rad has an intense fear of dolls.

    • He was born wingless due to a disability.
